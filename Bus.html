Kyoto Bus

Kyoto buses operate from approximately 6:00 to 23:00, though some run earlier or later depending on the route. At the Kyoto Station Bus Information Center you can get bus maps, buy tickets, and inquire about additional travel information. We recommend you ask an officer at the bus station or police box if you cannot find your destination or if you got lost. If you plan to be traveling by bus a lot, visit our Day Pass Tickets page for information about convenient day passes.

Bus Travel

Step 1: Get on the bus through the rear door.
* Highlighted by red color.

* Payment can be done by Ticket, Coin and IC Card.

Step 2: If you don't have any of the payment method mentioned above.
You can go at the front of the bus (Side of the driver) there is the machine. Where you can exhange the Money for coin.

* Please note that you should pay fare with exact amount.
=> Adults: ¥230
=> Children (under 12 years old): ¥120

Step 3: At the time to exit the bus, go to the machine (Which is also the exit way from the bus).
Make the payment.

Step 4: For coin Payment.
Make payment by exact amount in the Highlighted area.

* Highlighted by red color.

Step 5: For ticet payment.
Insert the ticket in the Highlighted area.

* Highlighted by red color.

Step 6: For IC Card payment.
Touch the IC Card in the Highlighted area.

* Highlighted by red color.

Step 7: Get of the bus through the front door.
* Highlighted by blue color.
